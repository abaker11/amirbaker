Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in each consulting session?

Each session includes personalized consulting, hands-on implementation guidance, custom strategies for your business, recorded session notes, and follow-up resources. You'll also get email support between sessions.

How do consulting sessions work?

All sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom/Meet) and are fully customized to your business needs. I provide screen sharing, hands-on guidance, and follow-up materials after each session.

What if I need more time after my session?

If we need additional time, you can book another session or upgrade to a package. I also offer brief email follow-up support for questions related to topics covered in our session.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Perfect! I specialize in consulting beginners and will start from the fundamentals. Each session is tailored to your current skill level, ensuring you learn at a comfortable pace.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with your consulting session, I'll refund your payment or provide a free follow-up session to address your concerns.
