Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my consulting services

How do consulting sessions work?

All sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom/Meet) and are fully customized to your business needs. I provide screen sharing, hands-on guidance, and follow-up materials after each session.

What's included in each hourly session?

Each session includes personalized consulting, hands-on implementation, custom strategies for your business, recorded session notes, and follow-up resources. You'll also get email support between sessions.

Do you offer package deals?

Yes! I offer 5-hour and 10-hour packages with discounted rates. These are perfect for comprehensive consulting programs and typically provide better value for extensive learning goals.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

Perfect! I specialize in consulting beginners and will start from the fundamentals. Each session is tailored to your current skill level, ensuring you learn at a comfortable pace.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients start implementing strategies immediately and see measurable improvements within 2-4 weeks. The timeline depends on your business type, current setup, and how quickly you implement the consulting.
